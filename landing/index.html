WhaReception

A lightweight WhatsApp receptionist for small and medium businesses.

WhaReception helps veterinary clinics and service-based businesses organize WhatsApp conversations, answer common questions, collect appointment requests, and route urgent cases to staff.

What it does

  • Answers frequently asked questions.
  • Collects appointment and service requests.
  • Detects urgent or emergency cases.
  • Sends structured summaries to business teams.
  • Helps staff respond faster when they are busy.

Built for service businesses

The first version is focused on veterinary clinics, but the platform is designed to support other local businesses such as aesthetic clinics, mechanics, nutritionists, barbershops, and restaurants.

Our approach

WhaReception does not replace business staff or existing appointment systems. It works as a simple layer in front of WhatsApp to organize incoming messages before a human confirms or responds.
